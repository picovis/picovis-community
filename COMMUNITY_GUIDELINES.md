## 📋 Community Standards

### ✅ **What We Encourage**

<details>
<summary><strong>🤝 Be Helpful & Supportive</strong></summary>

- **Answer questions** when you have knowledge to share
- **Share your experiences** and real-world use cases
- **Provide constructive feedback** on ideas and suggestions
- **Welcome newcomers** and help them get started
- **Follow up** on discussions you participate in

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>🎯 Stay On Topic</strong></summary>

- Keep discussions **related to Picovis**, database analytics, or AI
- Use **appropriate discussion categories** for your posts
- **Search before posting** to avoid duplicates
- **Link to related discussions** when relevant

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>💼 Be Professional</strong></summary>

- Use **clear, descriptive titles** for your posts
- **Provide context and details** in your questions
- **Follow up** on your questions and mark solutions
- **Respect others' time** and expertise
- **Use proper formatting** for code and logs

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>🔒 Share Responsibly</strong></summary>

- **Remove sensitive data** from examples and logs
- **Credit others' work** appropriately
- **Share knowledge** that benefits the broader community
- **Respect intellectual property** rights

</details>

### ❌ **What We Don't Allow**

<details>
<summary><strong>🚫 Disrespectful Behavior</strong></summary>

- **No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks**
- **Avoid inflammatory language** or trolling
- **Don't dismiss others' questions** or experiences
- **No gatekeeping** - everyone was a beginner once

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>📢 Spam or Excessive Self-Promotion</strong></summary>

- **No excessive self-promotion** or advertising
- **Don't post the same question** multiple times
- **Avoid off-topic commercial content**
- **No unsolicited direct messages** for promotion

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>🔐 Sensitive Information Sharing</strong></summary>

- **Never share passwords, API keys, or credentials**
- **Don't post sensitive business data**
- **Be careful with database schemas** and personal information
- **Redact sensitive information** from screenshots and logs

</details>

## 🆘 Getting Help

### 🔍 **Before Asking for Help**

<div align="center">

**Save time for everyone by checking these resources first!**

</div>

| Step | Resource | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| 1️⃣ | [📖 **Documentation**](https://github.com/picovis/picovis-community/wiki) | Comprehensive guides and references |
| 2️⃣ | [🔧 **Troubleshooting Guide**](https://github.com/picovis/picovis-community/wiki/Troubleshooting) | Common issues and solutions |
| 3️⃣ | [🔍 **Search Discussions**](https://github.com/picovis/picovis-community/discussions) | Look for similar questions |
| 4️⃣ | [🐛 **Check Issues**](https://github.com/picovis/picovis-community/issues) | See if it's a known bug |

### 📝 **When Asking Questions**

<details>
<summary><strong>✅ Include These Details</strong></summary>

- **Descriptive title** that summarizes your issue
- **Picovis version** (`picovis --version`)
- **Operating system** and version
- **Database type** and version (if applicable)
- **Complete error messages** (with sensitive data removed)
- **Steps to reproduce** the issue
- **Expected vs. actual behavior**
- **What you've already tried**

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>📋 Question Template</strong></summary>

```markdown
## Problem Description
Brief description of what you're trying to do and what's going wrong.

## Environment
- Picovis CLI Version: 
- Operating System: 
- Database Type: 
- Database Version: 

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Run command: `picovis ...`
2. Expected: ...
3. Actual: ...

## Error Messages
```
Paste error messages here (remove sensitive data)
```

## What I've Tried
- Checked documentation: [link]
- Searched discussions: [link]
- Tried: ...
```

</details>

### ⏱️ **Response Expectations**

- **Community volunteers** respond when available
- **Complex issues** may take longer to resolve
- **Be patient** and provide additional details if requested
- **Consider helping others** while waiting for help
- **Mark solutions** when your issue is resolved
